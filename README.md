# BRAVENxUNITED: Wings of Opportunity

A prototype website for a 1-day aviation discovery event designed to give middle school students of color exposure to aviation careers through mentorship, hands-on activities, and interactive learning experiences.

## ğŸ¯ Project Overview

**BRAVENxUNITED: Wings of Opportunity** is a free 1-day aviation discovery event for middle school students. This prototype website demonstrates the event structure, activities, and student journey, showcasing how students can explore aviation careers through:

- **Pilot mentors of color** who share their stories
- **Hands-on flight simulator** experiences
- **Interactive STEM activities** including aircraft building
- **Career pathway exploration** with clear next steps
- **Free event** with meals and transportation support
