Program Overview

How It Works

This interactive website guides you through an immersive aviation discovery experience. As you explore the different sections, you'll learn about aviation careers, try hands-on activities, and meet inspiring pilots.

At the end of your journey, when you submit your interest form, you'll be asked to complete a questionnaire about your experience. This helps us understand what you learned and how we can make the program even better for future students.
