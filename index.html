Program Overview

The Problem

Middle school students of color lack exposure, mentors, and aviation role models. Without seeing themselves represented in aviation careers, many never consider this path as a possibility.

Our Solution

A 1-day high-impact aviation exposure event built around awareness + mentorship. Students meet pilots of color, experience hands-on aviation activities, and discover clear pathways to careers in aviation.
