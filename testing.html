Testing Questions

Stakeholder feedback prompts for prototype evaluation

1. First Impressions

What was your first reaction when you saw this website? Did it make you excited about the event?

2. Clarity

Was it easy to understand what the event is about? What questions do you still have?

3. Activities

Which activities look most interesting to you? Would you want to try them?

4. Schedule

Does the schedule make sense? Is there anything you'd want to change about the day?

5. Representation

How important is it to you to see pilots who look like you? Does this website show that?

6. Motivation

After looking at this website, how interested are you in attending? What would make you more excited?

1. Trust & Safety

Does this website provide enough information for you to feel comfortable sending your child to this event? What additional information would you need?

2. Value Proposition

What value do you see in this program for your child? Is the free cost clearly communicated?

3. Logistics

Are the logistics (location, time, transportation) clearly explained? Do you have any concerns?

4. Mentorship

How important is it to you that mentors share your child's background? Is this clearly communicated?

5. Registration

Is the interest form easy to complete? Would you feel comfortable submitting it?

6. Follow-up

What information would you want to receive after expressing interest? How should the program communicate with you?

1. Program Understanding

Is the program's mission and approach clearly explained? Do you understand the impact goals?

2. Mentorship Structure

Does the schedule and mentorship approach seem effective? Would you want to participate as a mentor?

3. Activities

Are the activities appropriate and engaging? Do they effectively introduce aviation concepts?

4. Representation

Is the emphasis on mentors of color clearly communicated? Is this important to you?

5. Partnership

If you represent an organization, what information would you need to consider partnering? Is it on the website?

6. Improvements

What would you add or change to make this program more effective? What's missing?

1. Educational Value

Do you see educational value in this program? How does it align with your curriculum goals?

2. Student Selection

Would you recommend this program to your students? What criteria would you use to identify good candidates?

3. Integration

How could this program connect to classroom learning? What follow-up activities would be helpful?

4. Resources

What resources or information would you need to share this with students and parents? Is it available on the website?

5. Impact

Do the expected impact metrics seem realistic? What outcomes would you want to see?

6. Support

What support would you need from the program organizers to help promote this to your students?
